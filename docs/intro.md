## How It Works

### 1. Upload Your Project
Package your Unreal Engine project for Linux or Windows and upload to Third Space

### 2. Automatic Deployment
We detect your OS, provision GPUs, and configure streaming infrastructure

### 3. Share & Stream
Get your unique URL and embed anywhere with iframe. Users access via browser - no downloads!

### 4. Pay Only for Active Use
Credits consumed only when users are connected. Automatic idle timeout saves costs.

[Learn More About How It Works →](./core-concepts/how-it-works)
